About Mole Mania
Mole Mania is a whack-a-mole game. It was the first time I tried working with sound and VFX and learned how to incorporate them into projects in Unity. I also made an endless mode you can play instead of the story mode.
In Mole Mania, you are tasked to play a whack-a-mole game by a slezy-looking man. Where you can either play the game or fight back against the machine. I implemented all the sound effects, music, and the VFX in order to give that extra sense of catharsis.
I also took the base gameplay and made an endless mode out of it if you want to play the wack-a-mole game murder free. Where the game gets pergressivly more difficult as the game progresses.
